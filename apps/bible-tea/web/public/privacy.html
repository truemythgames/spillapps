Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Information We Collect

Bible Tea collects minimal information to provide our service. This includes anonymous usage analytics (e.g., which stories are played, app opens) to help us improve the experience. If you create an account, we store your email address and authentication data.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to understand app usage and fix bugs. These services may collect device information, app interaction data, and crash logs. No personally identifiable information is shared with these services beyond what is described here.

Third-Party Services

Bible Tea may integrate with third-party services including Apple Sign In, Google Sign In, and RevenueCat for subscription management. Each of these services has their own privacy policy.

Data Storage

Your listening progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. Account data is stored securely on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Bible Tea is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@bibletea.app.
